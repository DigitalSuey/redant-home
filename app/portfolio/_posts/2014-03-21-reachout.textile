h3. Project Description

ReachOut helps young people by improving their understanding of the issues that relate to mental health and wellbeing and providing information to better understand other people’s experiences.

Reach Out also has information on how to get the best help from services, as well as opportunities for young people to connect with other young people.

h3. Key sections:

  <ul>
    <li><b>Fact Sheets &amp; Stories:</b> provides information and stories on a range of mental health issues</li>
    <li><b>Forum:</b> where young people connect with the community through our online forums</li>
    <li><b>Blog: </b>for latest news, stories and opinion pieces from ReachOut.com</li>
    <li><b>Videos: </b>to browse video stories and content from other young people and ReachOut.com</li>
    <li><b>SMS Tips:</b> where users can sign up for SMS tip campaigns on areas of mental health and wellbeing</li>
    <li><b>Get Involved:</b> to access the latest opportunities, and all the tools and info to get involved in ReachOut.com!</li>
  </ul>
