Digital Development Workshop

Fire up your digital project with a Red Ant Digital Development Workshop. Bring your vision, goals and stakeholders together and create an actionable plan to take your idea to code.

Use our detailed planning processes that we have developed over 25 years of development to get your project started on the right track.

Let’s start now

Discovery Workshop + Product Roadmap + Sprint Plan

We have developed a package of services to take your vision and put it into code.

1. Discovery Workshop

Starting with a customised discovery workshop we bring stakeholders together to envisage the audiences, their goals and intelligent ways of supporting their experience. Our workshops are fun, challenging and get to the core of development priorities. You will leave knowing that your digital project is aligned with stakeholders and organisational goals. Creating the confidence and motivation to move forward

2. Product Roadmap

Following the Discovery Workshop we use the findings and produce them as a Product Roadmap. The Roadmap communicates the big picture - phases of development and how they align with business goals. It details testing phases and success criteria so you know you are on the right track. The roadmap can be shared with stakeholders, employees and the development team to keep everyone in the tent.

3. Sprint Plan

Finally a Sprint Plan is your actionable guide to getting it done. Using Agile methodology we break down the phases of development into 30 day Sprints detailing development deliverables. Your Sprint Plan will serve as a highly actionable guide that your development team can use to get started, and you can use to engage with critical test criteria along the way. A sprint plan avoids time and budget blow outs - you can make small corrections during the development phase. A well constructed sprint plan takes away many of the risks involved in a technical build as the complex areas, dependencies and gotchas can be identified before you begin.

At the end of this phase your will:

  • Know your project resources, time and costs required
  • Understand the complex parts and the project dependencies
  • Have a good idea when you will see a return and how to get their sooner
  • Be aligned on the development priorities and business goals
  • Orchestrate your remotely located development teams
  • Have engaged stakeholders and a motivated team ready to start development

Your Product Roadmap will give you the bigger picture of what your project will deliver. You will have a one page document that you can share with your stakeholders to outline the evolution of your digital product. How the needs of different user groups will be supported and the priorities for development.

Your Agile Sprint Plan will detail the phases of delivery into actionable 2 week sprints. You will clarity on what will be developed in each sprint and how it can be tested and confirmed. A Sprint Plan puts you back in control over your development and give you the ability to engage and make refinements during the development phase.

Preparing for your Discovery workshop

  • Prior to your workshop, send us your business goals, and models, current and desired customer groups, customer research and insights, website analytics, customer and employee feedback
  • Bring any insight or feedback from key stakeholder or bring them to the workshop
  • Bring your project budget and timeframe and any key dates or seasons
  • Bring an open mind, be prepared to be surprised and challenged and come away with a re-frame on an existing problem or a new solution you hadn’t considered.

About Agile Sprint Planning

Building a digital product can be daunting. Agile methodology was developed to help tech teams plan their work into short term “sprints” usually around 2 weeks. We tend to use a two week timeframe. This gives flexibility to adapt and make changes along the way. However if you’ve got a fixed budget or fixed timeline - then you’re going to need a bit more certainty.
